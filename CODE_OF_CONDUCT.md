# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `term-pcl` to be a respectful and productive project for users and contributors. Participants are expected to communicate professionally and focus on improving the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give constructive, specific feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Assume good intent while still addressing problems directly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusive or unprofessional conduct in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to Robolabs AI at contact@robolabs.ai. Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet these expectations.

This code applies in project spaces and in public interactions when representing the project.
